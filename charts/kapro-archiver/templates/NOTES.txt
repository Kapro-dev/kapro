kapro-archiver is installed as an opt-in CloudEvents archive receiver.

Point the Kapro operator CloudEvents sink at:

  http://{{ include "kapro-archiver.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc:{{ .Values.service.port }}/

For example:

  KAPRO_EVENTS_SINK_URL=http://{{ include "kapro-archiver.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc:{{ .Values.service.port }}/

The chart does not modify the operator deployment. Configure the operator sink
explicitly in your existing Kapro operator installation.
